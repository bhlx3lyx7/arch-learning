# Accounting Learning

## finance accounting

借: debit
贷: credit

借: 资产增加,负债和所有者权益减少
贷: 资产减少,负债和所有者权益增加

会计口诀:
- 有借必有贷，借贷必相等
- 资成费，借加贷减；收负所，借减贷加

### Debit balance accounts
debit increases, credit decreases
- asset
- expense
- dividends (exception)

### Credit balance accounts
credit increases, debit decreases
- liability
- equity
- revenue
